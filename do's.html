Do's

Drink plenty of fluids (water, coconut water, ORS solutions) to stay hydrated
Take acetaminophen/paracetamol for fever and pain as prescribed
Rest as much as possible to help your body recover
Monitor your temperature regularly
Watch for warning signs like severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, bleeding
Wear loose, comfortable clothing
Use mosquito repellents to prevent further mosquito bites
Use mosquito nets when sleeping, even during daytime naps
Follow your doctor's advice and attend all follow-up appointments
Check your platelet counts as recommended by your doctor
Eat small, frequent meals if you have poor appetite
Consume easy-to-digest foods like rice, toast, and soups
Keep your living area clean to prevent mosquito breeding
Remove standing water around your home
Accept help from family members during your recovery
Take prescribed medications at the scheduled times
Share your complete medical history with your healthcare provider
Keep cool compresses handy for fever spikes
Maintain good personal hygiene
Know the address and contact information of the nearest hospital
Inform your doctor if symptoms worsen
Consume foods rich in iron if recommended (like leafy greens, if tolerated)
Stay in screened or air-conditioned areas when possible
Keep a record of your symptoms, medication timing, and fluid intake
Allow for adequate recovery time before returning to normal activities
